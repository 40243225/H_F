Android 平台玩家領獎方法︰
1. 先登入神魔之塔
2. 跳出遊戲 ( 並非關閉遊戲 )，點擊領獎連結
3. 系統將跳回遊戲並彈出「獎品已經領取，請到"社群" -> "獎賞" 查閱」告示

iOS 平台玩家領獎方法︰
1. 先登入神魔之塔
2. 跳出遊戲 ( 並非關閉遊戲 )，複製連結到 Safari 瀏覽器使用
3. 系統將跳回遊戲並彈出「獎品已經領取，請到"社群" -> "獎賞" 查閱」告示

註1︰一人只限領獎一次

Redemption Method for Android:
1.Login TOS
2.Jump out the game (without closing the game) and hit the redemption link 
3.The system will direct you back to the game automatically and pop out a notice “The Reward is redeemed. Please confirm at " Community" —>”Rewards"

Redemption Method for iOS:
1.Login TOS
2.Jump out the game (without closing the game) and copy the redemption link to Safari browser 
3.The system will direct you back to the game automatically and pop out a notice “The Reward is redeemed. Please confirm at " Community" —>”Rewards"

Note 1: The rewards can only be redeemed once
